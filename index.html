FAQ

Common questions.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ is 125 units per 40HQ container. We do not offer single-unit or small-batch orders.

What brands and models are available?

We primarily stock KYMCO, Yamaha, and SYM in the 100cc–180cc range. Available inventory changes regularly — contact us for the current stock list.

How do you ensure the condition of the motorcycles?

Every unit is inspected before shipment. We provide pre-loading photos and a condition report for each order so you know exactly what you're getting before the container is sealed.

What documents do you provide for customs clearance?

We provide a full export document set: commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, and inspection report.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp or email with your target quantity and preferred brands. We'll send you the current stock list and a quotation within 24 hours.
